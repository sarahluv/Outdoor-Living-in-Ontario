OUTDOOR LIVING IN ONTARIO

POINTS OF INTEREST


Northern Ontario:

Five Mile Lake Provincial Park

1.5 square miles. Camping, fishing (walleye, northern pike, brook trout), canoing, self-guided nature trails.


SAFETY TIPS FOR CANOISTS

  • Be able to swim
  • Wear a life jacket at all times
  • Don't stand up or move around. If canoe tips,
    • Hang on to the canoe
    • Use the canoe for support and
    • Swim to shore
  • Don't overexert yourself
  • Use a bow light at night
